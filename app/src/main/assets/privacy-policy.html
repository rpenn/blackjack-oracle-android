Privacy Policy

1. Overview

We do not create user accounts, collect your name or contact information, or sell your data to anyone. Our apps are entertainment card and casino-style games that transmit only anonymized gameplay data — nothing that identifies you personally.

Angelfire Labs LLC ("Angelfire Labs," "we," "us," or "our") develops and publishes mobile games for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with all Angelfire Labs applications, including (but not limited to) Poker Oracle, our Blackjack game, and any future titles published under the Angelfire Labs name (collectively, "our Apps").

By downloading or using any Angelfire Labs App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Apps.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Our Apps do not require you to register an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or date of birth. We do not collect this type of information.

2.2 Gameplay Data (AI Advisor Feature)

When you use the AI Advisor feature (a premium subscription feature), your device sends a description of the current game state to our servers in order to generate personalized advice. This payload includes:

  • The cards dealt in the current hand (your hole cards and community cards)
  • The current game phase (e.g., pre-flop, flop, turn, river)
  • The pot size and betting actions taken during the hand
  • The number of active opponents
  • Your current hand classification (e.g., "pair of kings")

This data contains no personally identifiable information. It does not include your name, Google account, device identifier, IP address (which is processed transiently by our infrastructure provider and not logged or stored by us), or any other information that can be used to identify you as an individual. It is purely a snapshot of the game state at the time of your request.

Text responses generated by the AI Advisor are also passed to our text-to-speech service to produce spoken audio, which is returned to your device and played locally. We do not retain audio files after delivery.

2.3 Win Chance Feature

The Win Chance feature (a premium subscription feature) is computed entirely on your device using a Monte Carlo simulation. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party when you use this feature.

2.4 Payment Information

Premium features are sold as auto-renewing subscriptions through Google Play and processed exclusively by Google Play's billing system (Google Play Billing). We never receive, process, or store your payment card information, Google account credentials, or any billing details. All payment data is handled solely by Google, and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We receive only a transaction confirmation indicating whether a purchase was successfully completed.

2.5 Technical and Log Data

Our server infrastructure (hosted on Vercel) may automatically record standard technical log data when your device communicates with our API, such as the time of the request and the HTTP response code. These logs do not include your IP address in a form that is stored or linked to any individual, and are retained solely for server reliability monitoring. Logs are automatically purged in accordance with our infrastructure provider's standard data retention practices.

2.6 Analytics

We do not currently integrate third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar services) into our Apps. Google may collect aggregate, anonymized usage statistics through standard Google Play analytics (Google Play Console), which is governed by Google's own privacy policy and separate from our practices.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide the AI Advisor: Gameplay data is used solely to generate a relevant strategic response for the game state you submitted.
  • To deliver text-to-speech audio: The AI Advisor's text response is converted to speech and returned to your device.
  • To process and verify subscription purchases: Transaction confirmations from Google are used to unlock and maintain access to premium features.
  • To maintain server reliability: Technical log data is used to monitor the health and performance of our API.

We do not use your gameplay data to build profiles, target advertising, train machine learning models on your behalf, or for any purpose other than generating an immediate response to your request.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share data only as described below:

Recipient What Is Shared Purpose
AI Model Provider (accessed via our backend) Anonymized game state prompt Generating the AI Advisor's strategic response
Text-to-Speech Provider (accessed via our backend) The AI Advisor's text response Converting text to spoken audio

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, legal process, or a government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Angelfire Labs, our users, or the public.

5. Data Retention

Because we collect no personal information and do not maintain user accounts, we have no individual user data to retain or delete. Gameplay data transmitted for AI Advisor requests is processed in real time and is not stored in a database or linked to any identifier after the response is delivered.

Server access logs retained by our infrastructure provider are subject to that provider's standard retention schedule (typically 30 days or less) and are not associated with any individual user.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because our Apps do not require account creation or the submission of any personal information, the risk of inadvertent collection is minimal. If we become aware that a child under 13 has somehow provided personal information to us, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Our Apps contain simulated casino-style gameplay (poker, blackjack) for entertainment purposes and do not involve real money. However, parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise their children's use of our Apps and to configure parental controls on their devices accordingly. In-app subscription purchases require authorization through Google Play, which offers Google Play Family Library, parental controls, and purchase-approval settings.

7. Rights for Users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable national laws may give you specific rights regarding your personal data.

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information and do not maintain user accounts, most standard GDPR rights (such as data access, rectification, and erasure) are not applicable in practice — there is simply no personal data about you in our systems to act upon. However, you have the right to:

Right to Know

Receive confirmation of whether we hold personal data about you (we do not).

Right to Object

Object to any processing of personal data — though our processing is minimal and functional only.

Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time. The AI Advisor's data transmission can be avoided simply by not using that feature.

Our lawful basis for processing gameplay data is legitimate interests (providing the AI Advisor feature you have actively requested) and, where applicable, performance of a contract (delivering the premium subscription service you have purchased).

To exercise any GDPR rights, please contact us using the information in Section 10.

8. Rights for California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

Angelfire Labs does not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not sell personal information to third parties, and we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or sell (we collect none that identifies you).
  • Request deletion of personal information we have collected (no identifiable data exists to delete).
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share personal information).
  • Non-discrimination for exercising any CCPA/CPRA rights.

To submit a request or inquiry under CCPA/CPRA, please contact us using the information in Section 10.

9. Security

All data transmitted between our Apps and our backend API is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Because we do not store personal information or maintain user databases, the scope of our security obligations is narrow. We use commercially reputable infrastructure providers and follow industry-standard practices for securing our API endpoints.

No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of any data in transit. However, given the non-personal nature of the gameplay data we process, the risk to individuals from any such incident is minimal.
